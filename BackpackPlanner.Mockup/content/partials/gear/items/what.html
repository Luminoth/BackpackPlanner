What Is A Gear Item?

A Gear Item is a single piece of gear that is either carried, worn, or left in the car on a backpacking trip. Gear Items can also be consumable.

Some examples of Gear Items would be:

  • A backpack
  • A tent
  • Your favorite pair of shoes
  • A celebration beer waiting in the car

Gear Items are categorized by weight using the following scheme:

  • None
  • Ultralight
  • Light
  • Medium
  • Heavy
  • Extra Heavy
